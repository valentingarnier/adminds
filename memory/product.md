# Product Context

## The Problem
- Disability insurance reports ("rapports AI") are highly structured, legally complex documents
- Take psychiatrists 2-3 hours each to write
- Follow standardized forms from cantonal AI offices (Assurance Invalidité)
- Must align with Federal Tribunal case law (Foerster criteria, structured assessment indicators)
- Reports get sent back if non-compliant
- Doctors are poorly compensated for this work

## Target Users
- **Initial**: Psychiatrists in Geneva writing psychiatric AI reports
- **Stage**: Customer discovery with 5-10 psychiatrists as beta design partners

## Vision (Venture-Scale)
Transform clinical observations into compliant medico-legal documentation across:
- All report types (disability insurance, accident insurance SUVA/LAA, court reports)
- All medical specialties
- All European markets with similar systems (Germany, Austria, France)

## MVP Scope (v1.1 — March 2026)
- **Ingestion**: Multi-format upload — PDF, DOCX, images/scans, dictation input
- **Dossiers**: Patient dossiers with classified and reorganized documents
- **Report Generation**: AI-generated Rapport AI in DOCX format (optional PDF export)
- **Multi-cabinet**: Tenant isolation from day one — complete data separation
- **Compliance**: 100% Swiss hosting, LPD-compliant, no patient data leaves Swiss territory
